# Tidy Home Puerto Rico Website - Complete Build Specification for REPLIT Agent v2

## PROJECT OVERVIEW
Build a luxury home organization company website for Tidy Home Puerto Rico. The website should be modern, elegant, with a sage green color palette and cream backgrounds. It must be bilingual (English/Spanish), mobile-responsive, and include service modals, Instagram integration, and WhatsApp functionality.

## CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS
- **NO PRICING**: Never include specific prices. Only mention "per project pricing"
- **EXACT CONTENT**: Use only the provided content below - no modifications
- **BILINGUAL**: Full English/Spanish support with toggle
- **SERVICE MODALS**: All 6 services open in popups, not separate pages
- **WHATSAPP BUTTON**: Floating button on all pages linking to 19392021008
- **INSTAGRAM FEED**: Integration with @tidyhomepr

## TECH STACK
```
Framework: Next.js 14 with App Router
Styling: Tailwind CSS
Language: TypeScript
Deployment: Vercel
Forms: EmailJS or Formspree
Instagram: Instagram Basic Display API
Analytics: Google Analytics
```

```
Q: WHAT SERVICES DO YOU OFFER?
A: We specialize in home organizing, packing/unpacking, move coordination, space designing. Whether you're moving in, moving out, or simply looking for a more functional space, we've got you covered.

Q: HOW FAR IN ADVANCE SHOULD I BOOK YOUR SERVICES?
A: We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons. That said, if you're in a pinch, reach out. We'll do our best to accommodate you.

Q: HOW DO YOU CHARGE FOR YOUR SERVICES?
A: We charge per project rather than offering a flat rate because every family and space is unique, and we want to make sure our estimate reflects your specific needs. To do that, we start with a quick video call to get a better understanding of your space, goals, and any details that will help us create the most accurate and tailored proposal for you.

Q: HOW DO I GET STARTED?
A: Just fill out our contact form! We'll schedule a 15-minute call to get to know you and your needs.

Q: DO YOU HELP WITH UNPACKING AND SETTING UP MY NEW HOME?
A: Yes! We offer full unpacking and home setup services to help you get settled in quickly and comfortably. From kitchen organization to closet systems, we make your new space feel like home.

Q: DO YOU BRING YOUR OWN PACKING MATERIALS AND ORGANIZING SUPPLIES?
A: Yes, we offer an add-on service where we shop for all the materials needed for your project. Or, we're happy to work with whatever supplies you already have on hand.

Q: CAN YOU HELP WITH DOWNSIZING OR PREPPING FOR A MOVE?
A: Absolutely. We assist with sorting, donating and disposing of items before your move. We also help you plan and pack efficiently.

Q: WHAT AREAS DO YOU SERVE?
A: We serve Dorado, San Juan, Guaynabo and Bayamon. If you're outside our immediate area, contact us.
```
