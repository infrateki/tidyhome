# Tidy Home Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
**Company:** Tidy Home Puerto Rico  
**Tagline:** Your Space Matters  
**Industry:** Luxury Home Organization & Interior Services  
**Market:** Puerto Rico (Dorado, San Juan, Guaynabo, Bayamón)

## Brand Values
- **Calma** (Calm) - Creating peaceful, serene spaces
- **Orden** (Order) - Systematic organization and functionality
- **Calidez** (Warmth) - Personal, caring service
- **Frescura** (Freshness) - Modern, clean aesthetic
- **Limpieza** (Cleanliness) - Pristine, refined environments
- **Elegancia** (Elegance) - Sophisticated, luxury approach

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
```
Ebony (Calma)         #6B6F5D    RGB(107, 111, 93)
Sage (Orden)          #BDB982    RGB(189, 185, 130)
```

### Secondary Colors
```
Brown Sugar (Calidez) #C97456    RGB(201, 116, 86)
Ruddy Blue (Frescura) #94B5D6    RGB(148, 181, 214)
Alabaster (Limpieza)  #EDE7DD    RGB(237, 231, 221)
Kobicha (Elegancia)   #6B4F3C    RGB(107, 79, 60)
```

### Usage Guidelines
- **Backgrounds:** Alabaster (#EDE7DD) for primary, white for cards
- **Text:** Kobicha (#6B4F3C) for primary text, Ebony (#6B6F5D) for secondary
- **Accents:** Sage (#BDB982) for CTAs and hover states
- **Borders:** #D4D2CF for dividers, #E5E3E0 for subtle borders

## Typography

### Display Font - Headlines
**Font:** Marlide Display Light / Playfair Display Light  
**Weight:** 300  
**Letter Spacing:** 0.02em  
**Usage:** H1-H3 headings, hero text

### Body Font - Content
**Font:** Bicyclette Light/Regular / Inter  
**Weights:** 300 (light), 400 (regular)  
**Usage:** Navigation, body text, buttons

### Font Sizes
```
H1: 48-72px (responsive)
H2: 36-48px (responsive)
H3: 24-36px (responsive)
Body: 16px
Small: 14px
Navigation: 12px (uppercase, 0.15em tracking)
```

## Visual Style

### Photography
- Natural lighting, soft shadows
- Organized, clean spaces
- Lifestyle shots showing functionality
- Warm, inviting atmosphere
- Professional yet approachable

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous white space
- 2px borders for main dividers
- 12-16px padding increments
- Centered logo with balanced navigation
- Grid-based layouts for consistency

### UI Elements

#### Buttons
**Primary:**
- Background: Sage (#BDB982)
- Text: White
- Hover: Darken 10%
- Border-radius: Full (pills)

**Secondary:**
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 2px solid white/sage
- Hover: Fill with color

#### Cards
- Background: White
- Shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(107, 79, 60, 0.1)
- Border-radius: 12px
- Padding: 32px

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality
- Professional yet warm
- Sophisticated but approachable
- Calming and reassuring
- Expert and trustworthy
- Bilingual (English/Spanish)

### Messaging Pillars
1. **Transformation** - "Transform your space, transform your life"
2. **Expertise** - "Puerto Rico's premier organization service"
3. **Personalization** - "Tailored solutions for your lifestyle"
4. **Peace of Mind** - "Creating calm in every corner"

Hero
├── Video/Image background
├── Minimal text overlay
└── Dual CTA buttons

Services Grid

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
